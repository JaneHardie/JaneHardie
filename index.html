White Collar

White Collar is an American television show which ran for six seasons between 2009 and 2014.

It stars Matt Bomer as Neal Caffrey , a con artist who has evaded capture for years
until F.B.I. Agent Peter Burke , played by Tim DeKay, caught him.

With Caffrey facing years in prison, he asks Agent Burke to consider another option.
In exchange for his release, he would work for the F.B.I as a criminal informant and consultant.

Special Agent Peter Burke agrees to the deal and Neal Caffrey is released.
Neal is then introduced to the rest of Burke's team.

  • FBI Special Agent in Charge Reese Hughes ( played by James Rebhorn )
  • FBI Special Agent Clinton Jones ( played by Sharif Atkins )
  • FBI Special Agent Diana Berrigan ( played by Marsha Thomason )

The series also focuses on Neal Caffrey's love life. The three women in question are:

  1. Kate Moreau ( played by Alexandra Daddario )
  2. Alexandra Hunter ( played by Gloria Votsis )
  3. Sara Ellis ( played by Hilarie Burton )

Other regular characters in the show are Neal's best friend and sidekick Mozzie,
Peter's wife Elizabeth and Neal's landlady June.

A reboot was confirmed to be in the works on June 6, 2024.
